The Iron Ridge Compound

Iron Ridge isn't just a membership — it's a brotherhood. Set on 150 acres of private land, our compound is designed for men who want more than a typical gym or country club. This is a place to train hard, recover smart, learn new skills, and build lasting bonds.

From the clang of iron in the gym and the crack of rounds downrange, to the rip of FPV drones racing through the trees and the warmth of a fire pit under the stars — every corner of this property is built for purpose-driven men.

Explore the Compound

Eight distinct facilities spread across 150 acres — each built for a different part of the journey.

Iron Haven Gym

Fully loaded with premium free weights, plate-loaded machines, cardio rigs, and a dedicated functional training zone. This is the heart of the compound.

Free Weights Cardio Functional Training

The Cage & Mat

Professional grappling mats, MMA cages, heavy bags, and dedicated BJJ classes led by experienced instructors. Train striking, wrestling, and submissions.

BJJ Classes MMA Training Grappling Mats

Thunder Ridge Range

Multiple outdoor ranges including pistol, rifle, and tactical bays. Monthly competitions, certified safety courses, and marksmanship training for all skill levels.

Pistol & Rifle Tactical Bays Monthly Competitions

Air Zone

A dedicated FPV drone course winding through trees and obstacles. Equipment rentals, training for beginners, and monthly drone racing competitions with prizes.

FPV Training Rentals Available Monthly Races

The Recovery Lodge

Dry sauna, cedar hot tub, and a 50°F cold plunge. A dedicated space for contrast therapy, recovery from training, and mental clarity.

Sauna Hot Tub Cold Plunge

The Garage Bar & Grill

A rustic venue with a massive community fire pit, indoor dining, and an outdoor patio. Craft beer, wood-fired food, and a place to connect after a long day of training.

Community Fire Pit Indoor/Outdoor Craft Beer & Grill

Ridgeview Farm

A small family farm with goats, chickens, pigs, and horses. Open as a petting zoo for kids and families. A reminder that strength comes in many forms.

Petting Zoo Family Friendly Farm Animals

Community & Events

Weekly meetups, group training sessions, guest speakers, competition watch parties, and seasonal compound-wide events. This is where brothers become family.

Weekly Events Group Training Guest Speakers
