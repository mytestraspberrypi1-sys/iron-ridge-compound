The Mead Heritage Ranch Family Destination

Mead Heritage Ranch isn't just a membership — it's a family destination and a community. Set on 300 acres of private land, our property is designed for families who want more than a typical gym or country club. This is a place to train together, build skills, create lasting memories, and grow closer as a family — surrounded by other families on the same mission.

From strength training and martial arts to marksmanship, archery, swimming, basketball, tennis, and golf — every discipline is an opportunity to grow together. Weekend cookouts, bonfire gatherings, community events, and farm days bring everyone together. You'll grow stronger as individuals and closer as a family, rooted in a community that shares your values.

Explore the Destination

Fourteen distinct facilities spread across 300 acres — each built for a different part of the family journey.

Iron Haven Gym

Fully loaded with premium free weights, plate-loaded machines, cardio rigs, and a dedicated functional training zone. This is the heart of the compound.

Free Weights Cardio Functional Training

The Cage & Mat

Train boxing and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in a dedicated fight space with pro-grade rings, mats, and heavy bags. Classes for all levels — from first-timers to competitive fighters.

Boxing Classes BJJ Classes All Levels Welcome

Ranch Range

Multiple outdoor ranges including pistol, rifle, and tactical bays. Monthly competitions, certified safety courses, and marksmanship training for all skill levels.

Pistol & Rifle Tactical Bays Monthly Competitions

Air Zone

A dedicated FPV drone course winding through trees and obstacles. Equipment rentals, training for beginners, and monthly drone racing competitions with prizes.

FPV Training Rentals Available Monthly Races

The Recovery Lodge

Dry sauna, cedar hot tub, and a 50°F cold plunge. A dedicated space for contrast therapy, recovery from training, and mental clarity.

Sauna Hot Tub Cold Plunge

The Garage Bar & Grill

A rustic venue with a massive community fire pit, indoor dining, and an outdoor patio. Craft beer, wood-fired food, and a place to connect after a long day of training.

Community Fire Pit Indoor/Outdoor Craft Beer & Grill

Ridgeview Farm

A small family farm with goats, chickens, pigs, and horses. Open as a petting zoo for kids and families. A reminder that strength comes in many forms.

Petting Zoo Family Friendly Farm Animals

Community & Events

Weekly meetups, group training sessions, guest speakers, competition watch parties, and seasonal compound-wide events. This is where families find their people.

Weekly Events Group Training Guest Speakers

Heritage Lake

A private 6-acre lake with a wooden fishing pier, shaded sitting areas, and catch-and-release fishing. Perfect for quiet mornings with a rod or bringing the family to relax by the water.

Fishing Pier Family Friendly Catch & Release

Golf Course

A scenic 9-hole course with a full driving range and putting course. Perfect for family tee times, junior golf clinics, and weekend scrambles.

9-Hole Course Driving Range Junior Clinics

Archery

Four covered lanes with professional targets and beginner instruction. Master the bow as a family activity that builds focus and patience.

Covered Lanes Beginner Instruction Family Activity

Basketball Courts

Two full-size outdoor courts with night lighting. Run pick-up games, family tournaments, or work on your jumper under the lights.

Full-Size Courts Night Lighting Family Tournaments

Tennis Courts

Two Har-Tru hydro courts with professional surface. Lessons, clinics, and open play for all ages and skill levels.

Har-Tru Courts Lessons & Clinics All Skill Levels

Olympic Pool

A 50-meter heated Olympic pool with dedicated lap lanes, diving area, and kids' splash zone. Perfect for family swim time, lap swimming, and cooling off after a day on the ranch.

50-Meter Pool Diving Area Kids' Splash Zone
